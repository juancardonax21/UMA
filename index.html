📖 How were these items generated? / ¿Cómo se generaron estos ítems? ▸

The five GERS items were not formulated a priori by the authors. They emerged from a three-stage data-driven process applied to the consolidated academic literature on corporate reputation.
Los cinco ítems GERS no fueron formulados a priori por los autores. Emergieron de un proceso de tres etapas aplicado a la literatura académica consolidada sobre reputación corporativa.

1
Bibliometric mapping / Mapeo bibliométrico

948 articles on corporate reputation (Scopus, 1997–2018) were analyzed using VOSviewer. Keywords were extracted and mapped by co-occurrence frequency and link strength, yielding 44 core terms grouped into 7 thematic clusters representing the intellectual structure of the field.

948 artículos sobre reputación corporativa (Scopus, 1997–2018) se analizaron con VOSviewer. Las palabras clave se mapearon por frecuencia y fuerza de co-ocurrencia, generando 44 términos clave en 7 clústeres temáticos.

2
Semantic vector analysis / Análisis vectorial semántico (NLP)

Each keyword was embedded into a 384-dimensional vector space using the all-MiniLM-L6-v2 transformer model. For each cluster, a centroid vector was calculated and the keyword closest to it was identified as the semantic core of that cluster.

Cada término se incrustó en un espacio vectorial de 384 dimensiones con el modelo all-MiniLM-L6-v2. Para cada clúster se calculó un centroide y se identificó el término más próximo como núcleo semántico.

3
Consolidation and item formulation / Consolidación y formulación de ítems

The 7 clusters were consolidated into 5 macro-expectations. One item was formulated per macro-expectation, anchored in the semantic core of each cluster and designed to be interpretable by clients and non-clients alike.

Los 7 clústeres se consolidaron en 5 macro-expectativas. Se formuló un ítem por macro-expectativa, anclado en el núcleo semántico de cada clúster e interpretable por clientes y no-clientes.
