GERS Expert Panel Questionnaire

Global Expectations Reputation Scale — Content Validity Assessment
This questionnaire is bilingual (English / Spanish). Each item is presented in both languages.
Este cuestionario es bilingüe. Cada ítem se presenta en inglés y español.

You participated in a structured validation process during the development of the theoretical model underlying this scale. At that time, items were presented as candidate indicators of institutionalized stakeholder expectations in corporate reputation assessment, as part of a broader model validation exercise. This questionnaire formalizes that process for academic documentation purposes.

Participaste en un proceso de validación durante el desarrollo del modelo teórico que subyace a esta escala. En aquel momento, los ítems se presentaron como indicadores candidatos de las expectativas institucionalizadas de los stakeholders. Este cuestionario formaliza ese proceso para su documentación académica.

The scale — GERS (Global Expectations Reputation Scale) — operationalizes corporate reputation as stakeholders' perceived alignment with institutionalized expectations: the normative standards defining legitimate organizational conduct. Your responses are anonymous. Only aggregate statistics will be reported.

La escala GERS operacionaliza la reputación corporativa como el alineamiento percibido con las expectativas institucionalizadas. Tus respuestas son anónimas.
